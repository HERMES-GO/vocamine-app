What Vocamine offers

Vocamine is a vocabulary learning app built around science-backed review. It currently supports English and French learning with personalized daily goals, CEFR-based difficulty, spaced repetition, pronunciation, example sentences, and word-form support.

Core study flows are designed to work offline after vocabulary data has been loaded on your device, so you can keep learning without a stable connection.

FAQ

Why am I not seeing new words?

You may have finished the current CEFR level for your learning language, or today's queue may already be complete. Check your level and daily goal in the app profile, then continue with the next level or additional learning.

Why is audio unavailable?

Pronunciation depends on voices installed on your device. If playback does not start, open the voice settings in the app and install an offline voice for the learning language.

Can I switch between English and French?

Yes. Vocamine keeps language-specific settings, so your level, daily goal, notifications, and progress can be managed separately.

Does the app work offline?

Yes for core study after vocabulary data is available on the device. Some account, sync, and recovery features still require connectivity.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are configured per learning language. You can enable or change them from the profile screen inside the app.
